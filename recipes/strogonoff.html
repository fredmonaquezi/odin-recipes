Strogonoff!

Description:

This recipe is going to show you how to prepare the famous Brazilian Strogonoff dish.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Tbs of olive oil
  • 1 kilo of chicken cut in cubes
  • 2 Tbs of tomate sauce
  • 2 Tbs of mustard
  • 2 Tbs of ketchup
  • Mushroons to taste
  • 1 can of milk cream
  • 1 Onion
  • Salt

How to prepare:

  1. In a pan, put 3 Tbs of olive oil. Wait until it is hot and put the onion. Wait until the onion is cooked.
  2. Add 1 kilo of chicken cut in cubes and let it cook until it is crispy
  3. After that, add salt to taste, add 2 Tbs of tomato sauce, 2 Tbs of mustard, 2 Tbs of ketchup and mushroons, and mix it all together.
  4. Turn off the stove and add 1 can of milk cream and stir everything
  5. Serve it with rice and crispy potatoes
