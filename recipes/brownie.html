Brownies and more brownies!

Description:

Now let's cook something to sweeten or bodies! It's an easy and delicious recipe that everyone is able to do!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

How to prepare:

  1. Step 1:

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 baking dish.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Combine the melted butter, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each, until thoroughly blended.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Sift the flour, cocoa powder, and salt in a bowl. Gradually stir flour mixture into the egg mixture until blended. Stir in the chocolate morsels. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared baking dish.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Bake in preheated oven until an inserted toothpick comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Remove, and cool pan on wire rack before cutting.
